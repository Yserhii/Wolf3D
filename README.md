## Control menu:
 - W or ⬆ (move forward)
 - S or ⬇ (move back)
 - D (strive for the right)
 - A (strive for the left)
 - ⬅ or move mouse left (turn left)
 - ➡ or move mouse rigth (turn right)
 - Left Shift (speed up)
 - E (alcohol effect)
 - ESC (close window with game)
